About Me

I'm Shashank Raj (also known online as Spoiled Unknown ).
I'm a self-taught developer from India, and programming started as something I picked up out of curiosity and as a hobby. Over time, it slowly grew into something much bigger for me. I enjoy building things that are useful, interesting, or simply fun to create, especially tools and projects that can help people in some way.

My journey started back in 2019 with small web games. Since then, I've explored different areas including Web and Game development, graphics programming, and general software projects. These days I primarily focus on web applications using MEVN, and games with Unity. while still enjoying experiments in other areas whenever something catches my interest. Most of the projects and experiments I work on can be found in my project section.

Outside programming, I also enjoy exploring things beyond code. I occasionally spend time on Video Editing, Graphics Design/Photo Editing, hardware tinkering, and experimenting with random ideas that seem interesting. I'm always curious about learning new things, even if they're completely outside my usual field.
